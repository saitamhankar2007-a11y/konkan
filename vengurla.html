Vengurla Bandar

Vengurla Bandar (Port) refers to the historic port town of Vengurla in Sindhudurg, Maharashtra, known for its beautiful beaches, natural harbor, and significant past as a trading center established by Dutch and British traders, featuring landmarks like Dutch warehouses and the Vengurla Point Lighthouse, attracting tourists to its serene coastline and natural beauty. Key Aspects: Location: Sindhudurg district, Konkan coast, Arabian Sea. Historical Significance: A natural port since the 1600s, developed by Dutch and British for trade. Attractions: Vengurla Beach, Vengurla Fort, Dutch Wakhar (warehouse), St. Lukes Hospital, Crowferd Market, and the Vengurla Point Lighthouse. Vibe: Peaceful, clean, known for white sand beaches and surrounding hills. Accessibility: Nearest railway is Kudal (22 km), accessible via NH-17. In essence, Vengurla Bandar is the historic port area of Vengurla, a charming coastal destination in Maharashtra.
